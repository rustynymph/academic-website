Annie Kelly
annie.kelly@colorado.edu

Hi there, I'm a M.S. Creative Technologies and Design student in the ATLAS Institute at the University of Colorado Boulder. I also have a B.S. in Computer Science and a certificate in Technology, Arts, and Media from CU. I will graduate with my Masters in December 2019. I currently work as a researcher and developer in the Laboratory for Playful Computation.

I work on designing programmable technologies that are accessible to non-technical populations, with a specific focus on technologies for live performance art.

In addition to my research, I am usually working on several artistic endeavors at a time which include interactive and immersive installations and playing in loud obnoxious rock bands.
