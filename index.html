Hi, I'm Annie.

I am a designer with a passion for all things creative and interactive. I have an M.S. in Creative Technologies and Design, and a B.S. in Computer Science. I have a unique combination of design, development, and research expertise that enable me to tackle a diverse set of user experience projects.
