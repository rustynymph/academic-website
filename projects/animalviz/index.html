Focus group: An invesigation into family's scientific curiosities about their pets' senses (May 2018)

In 2018, my collaborators and I led a focus group to investigate what curiosities and questions families, particularly adolescents, have about their pets' senses and experiences. We facilitated discussion among the group with questions we designed ahead of time, as well as a post-it based activity where families could write down responses and questions to prompts we wrote on the board.
