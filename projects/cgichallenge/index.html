Design Challenge
Prompt: Freshmen Engagement

"Attending a new school can create a lot of stress and challenges for freshman. Design an experience that helps new students get oriented, choose courses that are well regarded and align with their interests. Consider how they might navigate their day and engage with their new surroundings."

Value Proposition

A technology-supported experience that attends to the goals and anxieties of students at Freshmen orientation.

Use Cases


Exploring campus

Help students get acquainted with campus by exploring their surroundings and visiting key locations to collect passports to gain campus cash rewards.


"Find someone who" icebreaker

Facilitate socializing among students through icebreakers that get people to move around campus and learn about their peers.


Course catalog & registration

Aid students in finding courses that fulfull their course requirements, align with their interests, and are worthwhile.
