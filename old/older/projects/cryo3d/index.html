Cryo3D

Interactive 3D Visualizations of Snow and Ice Data

Cryo3D is a web based tool that provides interactive 3D visualizations of Arctic data. Cryo3D was a Computer Science Capstone 2015 project sponsored by the National Snow and Ice Data Center (NSIDC) in Boulder, CO.

Cyro3D offers automated tours through 3D terrain showcasing specific areas of NSIDC interest, and had Oculus Rift support. Cryo3D won best demo at the Senior Capstone Expo.
