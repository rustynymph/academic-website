Authentic Expression Counseling, LLC (completed in 2019)

Authentic Expression Counseling is a therapy private practice located in Longmont, CO. The owner of the company wanted to get a website up and running as quickly and cheaply as possible. I helped her purchase a domain for her site, and built everything using custom HTML and CSS on top of a Bootstrap template.

Inner Wisdom Therapy (completed in 2018)

Inner Wisdom Therapy is a therapy private practice located in Boulder, CO. I helped the owner of the company design her website using Squarespace. My work included designing a logo, designing the landing page, and helping create content for different sections of the page.

Shaping the Future (completed in 2012)

Shaping the Future is a child care center in Massapequa, New York. I designed the logo for Shaping the Future and they hired someone to paint my design on the front of the building. They wanted a design that was bright and playful.
